The NVSS & SUMSS Catalogues


Here you can browse a joined database of NVSS and SUMSS radio sources and investigate objects that may be suitable for ASKAP commissioning/early science surveys. These have been matched by position, with a match determined to exist for sources within a 10 arcsecond radius. AT20G data has also been included, as well as a link to the corresponding position search on NED for each object when queried.

Compiled by Marcin Glowacki, with much help from Dr. Cormac Purcell.

Position search

Compact sources (|Alpha| < 0.5)

Display sources within NVSS that were also found in at least two other sources and had a SED fitted and found to have a spectral index, alpha, of between -0.5 and 0.5. Such sources may be compact. You can view the SEDs used in a pdf file, and restrict the results based on reduced chi-square value for the fit (maximum of 70) , and whether it was flagged as a good fit by eye or not (default is for good fits only; if including bad fits please inspect the SED file).

Objects within the Bright Extra-galactic Radio Source Catalogue

Display objects within NVSS and/or SUMSS that has a corresponding component within the Bright Extra-galactic Radio Source (BERS) catalogue (Kuhr et al., 1981). Each object in this catalogue was found to have a total flux density minimum of 1 Jy. Matches were made beforehand on position in RA and DEC. The class of object and its spectral index (alpha) determined by BERS is included. A link to the flux readings for each BERS object will also be provided soon.
